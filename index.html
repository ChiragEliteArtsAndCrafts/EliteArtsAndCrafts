I · Who we are

Young minds.
Bold designs.
Global dreams.

Since 2016
Jodhpur, Rajasthan

Elite Arts & Crafts began its journey in furniture manufacturing and export in 2016 - with a humble start.

Known for a young, dynamic team with a can-do attitude, the company quickly rose to become one of Jodhpur's leading furniture exporters.

Our workforce comprises between 600 and 800 skilled personnel - artisans, operators, supervisors - all committed to creating art.

Our rapid growth is driven by strict quality protocols and robust internal controls, ensuring consistent delivery of high-quality furniture piece after piece.

II · Where the magic happens

Built to build.
Faster. Smarter.
Without compromise.

Four pillars
One discipline
01 · Machinery
Advanced woodworking

Multiple automatic woodworking setups, including high-frequency jointing machinery for ultra-speed jointing.

02 · Finishing
Precision handling

Movement across sanding & finishing stations on rollers - ensuring impeccable surface perfection, line to crate.

03 · Metal
Powder-coated metalwork

An advanced metal facility with an on-site powder-coating plant - consistent, durable, production-scale finishes.

04 · Timber
In-house seasoning

Over 200,000 CFT of timber, seasoned and chemically treated in our own plant - ready when the order lands.

One standard
Green energy

An integrated 2 MW solar plant reduces our carbon footprint - and our customers' landed emissions.

Certifications

SEDEX, FSC, EUDR, VRIKSH and more - the paperwork that lets our pieces cross fifteen borders without friction.
